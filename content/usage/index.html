USGIN

United States Geoscience Information Network

Usage

The following links are intended to help USGIN users:

  • Using USGIN: An overview of the many ways USGIN can be used
  • USGIN Data Provider Workflow: A synopsis of the process undertaken by technical users who wish to share their data according to USGIN specifications
  • USGIN Best Practices: A list of recommended standards and practices for sharing data according to USGIN specifications
  • USGIN Specifications (About): Links to a list of USGIN specifications and an entry in the USGIN FAQ that describes USGIN specifications
  • USGIN Software Applications: Software applications created, used, and supported by USGIN

Funding for the USGIN initiative provided by the National Science Foundation http://nsf.gov under NSF award number EAR-0753154, as part of the INTEROP initiative. The USGIN initiative is managed by the Arizona Geological Survey (AZGS) [Dead Link, http://azgs.az.gov] on behalf of the Association of American State Geologists (AASG). Some USGIN documents and products were developed for use by the National Geothermal Data System [Dead Link, http://www.geothermaldata.org] (NGDS), which is powered by USGIN; these materials are supported by the Department of Energy under grants EE-0002850 and EE-0001120 awarded to the AASG and Boise State University, respectively.
